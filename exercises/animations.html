Practice: CSS Transition + Animation

Hover or focus each button. The paragraph drops down over 1 second (transition), while the buttons cycle through three colors over 5 seconds (animation).

  • Home

    Home shows an overview. This paragraph is revealed by a 1s transition that changes max-height, opacity, and transform.

  • About

    About explains your mission. Using li:hover .dropdown and li:focus-within .dropdown supports mouse and keyboard.

  • Services

    Services lists what you offer. The button’s background runs a 5s, infinite, alternating 3-color animation via @keyframes.

  • Contact

    Contact has ways to reach you. Try tabbing to focus the items—no mouse needed.
